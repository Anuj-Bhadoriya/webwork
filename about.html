About us

Give a helping hand to a needy people

KalaVyom Foundation is dedicated to advancing art, culture, skill development, experiential learning, capacity building and tourism initiatives to create a brighter future for India’s next generation. Our mission is to empower emerging talents by providing platforms for artistic expression, skill-building opportunities, and cultural leadership. Since its inception, KalaVyom has been committed to bringing India’s rich artistic heritage to the masses, shifting the experience from closed galleries to vibrant public spaces. Our dynamic initiatives include workshops, lectures, and training programs in music, acting, and various other art forms, all designed to inspire creativity, develop skills, and foster tourism. A cornerstone of our approach is capacity building—enhancing the abilities of both staff and communities to drive impactful and sustainable programs. By integrating training modules and empowerment strategies, we aim to equip young artists and underrepresented communities with the tools to thrive in the arts and beyond. Our efforts are strengthened by collaborations with renowned artists and experts who bring their technical expertise and experience to our initiatives. Over the years, we’ve had the privilege of hosting respected figures, including Madhya Pradesh Governor Mangubhai Patel, filmmaker Dilip Shukla, actor Alok Chatterjee, Padmashree awardee Kaluram Bamaniya, National School of Drama Director Chittaranjan Tripathi many others, enriching our programs and inspiring the next generation of creators. At KalaVyom Foundation, we continuously evolve to meet the changing needs of society, fostering an environment where art becomes a medium of empowerment, cultural exchange, and skill development. Join us in building a future where creativity and opportunity go hand in hand.

From the vibrant streets of Delhi to the serene shores of Andaman and the cultural heartlands of Rajasthan and Gujarat, Kalavyom has consistently pushed boundaries, exploring new frontiers in creativity and collaboration. We aim to not only nurture the next generation of artists but also provide them with meaningful career opportunities, transforming passion into livelihood.

Our Vision

To foster a nationwide culture of learning, performing, and creating that drives meaningful change in the arts, culture, skill development, and tourism sectors. We achieve this by empowering and engaging local communities to shape their artistic futures.

Learn More

Our Mission

To make art accessible and relevant to today’s youth, bridging the gap by bringing art to them rather than expecting them to seek it.

Learn More

As we move forward, Kalavyom is committed to empowering India’s all for all communities through cultural programs that celebrate their rich traditions and foster resilience. By integrating capacity-building initiatives, we strive to preserve and promote indigeneity by supporting traditional art forms, storytelling ans performance. Our efforts also focus on creating opportunities for cultural leadership by training artists and artisans to become cultural ambassadors, ensuring their voices are heard on national and international platforms. We aim to facilitate sustainable livelihoods by designing programs that merge artistic expression with skill development, offering pathways for economic self-reliance. Indigeneity lies at the heart of our mission—not merely as a celebration of heritage but as a vital tool for social and economic empowerment. Together, we can build a future where every community thrives through its unique cultural identity.
